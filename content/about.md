# About me

Hi! My name is **Liang Qiu 邱亮** (pronounced as Leon Chew).

I'm a researcher focusing on **language model alignment** and **reinforcement learning**. I was a Senior Applied Scientist at Amazon and earned my Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from [UCLA](http://www.ucla.edu/), advised by [Prof. Song-Chun Zhu](http://www.stat.ucla.edu/~sczhu/) and [Prof. Achuta Kadambi](https://www.ee.ucla.edu/achuta-kadambi/).

## Research Interests
